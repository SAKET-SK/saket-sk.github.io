A full-stack developer is a web developer or engineer who works with both the front and back ends of a website or application—meaning they can tackle projects that involve databases, building user-facing websites, or even work with clients during the planning phase of projects..

Tenure Highlights:

- Learnt the basic concepts of web development i.e HTML,CSS,JavaScript Basics.
- Observed key points and types of elements used in making a Website.
- Got to learn many new concepts and it proved to be a very knowledgeable journey.
- Performed a variety of activities and learnt basics of web development. Learnt how to apply the basics of design and make UI more interactive for users.
- Gained the practical implementation of the concepts learned and their usage and impact in the real world.
- Worked in a group of 6 and created following Frontend Projects: Wedding Planner, Hospital Website, Grocery Store and Interior Designer’s Website; thus, reflecting amazing skills in the field of UI Design. (No Database Included)

Event Details

The internship was intended for an aspiring developer who is willing to work as a Full Stack web developer. The teachings were perfect and administration was near perfect. The company was small scale but trained many interns in various fields of IT and Software Development.


Overall interns were assigned work from various clients of the company and suitable guidance was provided by the higher level employees. It was very informative throughout the journey.


Conclusion

This internship was successful and instilled many web development concepts that were ready to use and moreover gave the interns confidence that they will able to work upon more complex issues later on.
