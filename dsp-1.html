Online Program for Game Development (FreeLance)

About Work

Date- February 2021 - Present

Website-Click to visit

Mode - Online

An online program offered by XAMK South Eastern Finland University of Applied Sciences, primarily focusing on the basics of Game Development.

Tenure Highlights:

- An online program offered by XAMK covers all main topics related to Game development.
- An essential part is that the person who wants to make a carrier in Game development can take part and the teachable material will fully related to this field.
- From history of games, Analysis, Tools and Values etc. every base has been covered successfully.
- Apart form the basic knowledge, the course offers 25 ECTS (European Credit Transfer System) which might help the students to continue directly from 25 ECTS Credits out of 100, if wanting to study in Europe.
- Comprehensively worked on Design tools and made some of the designs assigned by the organization and submitted it to them regularly to obtain ECTS (European Credit Transfer System).
- Detailed Notes were made on the basis of what things were learnt.
- Comprehensive hands-on various software tools such as Unity 3D, Krita, Blender, etc.

Event Details

The program garnered the attention of many aspiring Game developers around the world. The instructor Prof. Jan Stongards instilled his knowledge and expertise about Game Development basics was amazing.


Platform that was used was TEACHABLE and assignments were given at the end of each module. After evaluation of the assignments, ECTS were assigned to the records of the respective student.


Conclusion

This internship was successful and instilled many web development concepts that were ready to use and moreover gave the interns confidence that they will able to work upon more complex issues later on.
